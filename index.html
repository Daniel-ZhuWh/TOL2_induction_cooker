How do induction cookers work?

Induction cookers can magically heat food without using fire. Do you know how it works?

Welcome! This is a course about how an induction cooker works. There will be three people in your team. And each one of you will be assigned a video clip to study a part of the working mechanism of induction cooker (three parts in total). Then you will teach each other what you have learned. Afterward, you will have a recap. In the end, all three of you will take a final test that covers the knowledge of all three parts. Your final score will be the average score of your group. Good luck!
